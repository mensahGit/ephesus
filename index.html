Fighting To Maintain His Composure...

It was late.

The cafe was closing soon.

He thought to catch an Uber. Then she walked in, smelling just like a fresh, store-bought pack of honey-maple bacon. It was uncanny. His hunger panges grew. The subtle moan of his lower abdomen grew into something much stronger. Murmur to moan it grew. And as she drew closer to him, that moan exploded into a thunderous roar.

Too embrassed to speak, he withdrew to the corner with coffee in hand.
